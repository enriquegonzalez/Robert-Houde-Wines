Portfolio

We represent Marc de Grazia selections of Italy, Terry Theise Estate selections in Champagne, Austria and Germany, Europvin selections from France and Spain, and many of our own direct European imports.

About Us

We are an independent importer and distributor of the finest artisanal wines. Founded in 2009, owned and operated by partners Robert Houde and Mark Hutchens. We started with 30 small wineries from Italy, and now represent over 180 estates across Europe and North America.

With 50 years of experience combined between us, we continue to source unique properties that are characteristic of their origins, and exemplify quality and distinctiveness.

Services

In addition to sales, we provide expert staff training, assistance in wine list creation and development, and host educational events to introduce and promote exciting properties to the top restaurants and wine shops in Chicagoland and Central Illinois areas.
